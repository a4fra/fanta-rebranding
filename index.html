ABOUT THE REBRAND

The Fanta Rebrand Project was created to give the brand a fresh, modern identity that appeals to teenagers and young adults. The new design focuses on vibrant colours, bold typography, and a strong digital presence across social media platforms.

This concept keeps Fanta's fun and energetic personality while making the brand feel more contemporary and engaging for a new generation of consumers. The goal is to create a memorable experience through updated visuals, marketing, and product presentation.

Key features of the rebrand include:

  • Modern and eye-catching visual design
  • Stronger social media engagement
  • Youth-focused branding strategy
  • Improved digital marketing campaigns
  • Consistent brand identity across platforms
